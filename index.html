Welcome to the Bikini Bottom

The Krusty Krab is the heart of Bikini Bottom, where the most delicious Krabby Patties are made. This fast-food restaurant, shaped like a giant crab trap, has been the top eatery in town for years. The Krusty Krab is run by Mr. Krabs, a money-obsessed crab who would do anything to make a profit. Despite his greed, he’s still somewhat of a lovable character, especially when it comes to his secret formula for the Krabby Patty, which he guards with his life. The restaurant’s most enthusiastic employee is SpongeBob SquarePants, who works as the fry cook. SpongeBob loves his job more than anything, flipping Krabby Patties with a big smile on his face. His motto, “I’m ready, I’m ready, I’m ready!” is his way of expressing his unending optimism and determination. The Krusty Krab is always busy with customers lining up for a taste of SpongeBob’s perfect patties, but it's also the setting for a lot of comedic mishaps, especially when Plankton, the owner of the rival Chum Bucket, comes up with his latest scheme to steal the secret formula.
